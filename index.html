About Me

Hey there! My name’s Patrick and I’m learning to become a fullstack developer and exercise my creative gears! If you ever met me, you might think I’m like the Big Bang: an explosion of energy that makes something out of nothing! I am a very energetic young man with a passion for creating, building, developing, especially when it comes to art and technology. I’m sure you could agree that technology has created a whole new dimension when it comes to all forms of media by providing new mediums, and increasing interconnectivity. These types of ideas blow my mind and give me a dopamine rush the same way that finally wiring up an app does. There’s just something about the moment when your functions work correctly that keep you wanting more to do and more to learn! I like to think of coding as my “left-brain exercise,” and to balance out my brain I’m also learning to play guitar, I produce music digitally, write, paint, draw, oh man, I like to dip my hands in just about anything and see what I can do! And I think it’s very important to note how much I love people and enjoy working with them. You all are so very unique, interesting and just fun! I think this is sufficient, Patrick out !
